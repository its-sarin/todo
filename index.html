listify.io

Listify your life!

Listify.io is the fast and easy way to organize your life via 100% free (no signup or login necessary), easy-to-use online lists that are completely collaborative, allowing you to share your lists with friends, family, or a significant other simply by sharing the link to your list. Perfect for shopping, planning, coordinating, and organizing, Listify.io lists work great both at home and on your mobile device.

Getting started is as easy as creating your first list:

100% Free

No fees or subscriptions, no email addresses or passwords- Listify.io makes it easy to create and share an infinite number of lists without ever needing to log in.

Collaborative

Share a shopping list with your siginificant other, coordinate a group project, plan a party. With Listify.io, creating and sharing editable lists has never been easier.

Mobile

Listify.io was built to be mobile first. Create your lists at home then take them with you on your mobile device or create your lists on your mobile device then take them home.
