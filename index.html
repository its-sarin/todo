Listify your life!

Listify.io is a fast and easy way to organize your life via completely free (no signup or login necessary), easy-to-maintain online lists that are entirely collaborative, allowing you to share your lists with friends, family, or a significant other simply by sharing the link to your list. Perfect for shopping, planning, coordinating, and organizing, Listify.io lists work great both at home and on your mobile device.
